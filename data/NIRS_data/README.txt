## Acquisition of NIR data:
Absorbance at each wave number within a range of 12489,6 to 3594,9 cm-1 collected by a BRUKER MPA spectrometer.
Each spectrum is an average of 64 spectral readings from the same 51 mm or 22 mm diameter section, chosen according to the amount of material available for each sample.
